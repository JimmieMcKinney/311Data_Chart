Wealthier Residents of Queens, NY are More Likely to Call 311

By. Jimmie McKinney

Nov. 11, 2021

The five boroughs receive many complaints through its 311-call service. In just the Queens borough over 540 thousand 311 calls have been lodged since the beginning of the year; and there seems to be a pattern. Zip codes with the highest median income tend to call 311 more. Regardless of population and median age, the zip codes in Queens with the highest median income tend to report more 311 calls than their counterparts.
